WAW Map Tactics is all about Call of Duty: World at War maps and tactics for each map.

Features

13 HD quality maps for all the standard maps of the game.

Maps can be zoomed in and out either by pinching or double-tapping.

Tips and tactics for every map whether you are a run 'n gun type, or a camper, or sniper or ninja.

Will add videos soon featuring tactics from famous players of the WAW game.
